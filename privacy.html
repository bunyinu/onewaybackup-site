Privacy Policy

OneWay Backup · Last updated 23 June 2026

OneWay Backup (“OneWay”, “we”) is built so that your photos stay yours. Your photos and videos are never sent to us — they stay in your own Apple account. The only information collected is what’s needed to run your subscription, described below.

What we collect

Your photos and videos are never collected. They are copied into your own Apple account and never reach us — we run no servers that receive or store them.

The only data collected is to operate subscriptions. When you subscribe, our subscription provider RevenueCat receives your purchase history and an app/device identifier in order to manage your subscription and provide us aggregate subscription analytics. This data is never used for advertising, never sold, and never shared with data brokers.

Your photos and videos

OneWay keeps an independent, protected copy of your photos and videos in your own private iCloud, inside your Apple account. They never pass through us and we cannot see them. This copy is independent of your synced library, so it is not removed when you delete the original. We never delete from this backup — the app contains no code that can remove your backed-up files.

Sign in with Apple

We use Sign in with Apple to let you into the app. Apple may provide a user identifier, and (the first time, if you allow it) your name and email. This is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us.

Photo library permission

The app requests access to your photo library to back up your photos and videos, and to add them back when you choose to restore. Access is used only for these features. OneWay does not read your documents, messages, or other apps’ data.

Deleting your account and data

You can delete your account anytime in Settings → Account → Delete Account. This removes your Sign in with Apple identity and the app’s local data from your device. Your backed-up photos and videos in iCloud are not deleted — they remain in your own iCloud and you can sign back in to see them, or remove them yourself from your iCloud storage.

Tracking and third parties

We do not track you and use no advertising SDKs. We do not link your data with third-party data for advertising, and we share nothing with data brokers. We rely on two service providers, used only as described above: Apple (Sign in with Apple and App Store payments) and RevenueCat (subscription management and analytics).

Children

The app is not directed at children. We collect no data beyond what is needed to run your subscription, as described above.
